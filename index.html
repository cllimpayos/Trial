01 — the basics

About Me (the unfiltered version)

Hi, I'm Charlize — born September 1, 2000, which by my calculations makes me exactly old enough to have a Master's degree and absolutely no idea what a mortgage is. I did the whole education thing properly: Bachelor's degree, then a Master's degree, because apparently one piece of paper wasn't enough to confuse my future.

Plot twist nobody saw coming (except everyone, because it's very 2020s): I don't currently work in my degree field. Instead, I manage a frozen yoghurt shop in Australia, where I've become fluent in topping diplomacy, rush-hour triage, and the ancient art of smiling while the swirl machine makes a concerning noise.

I'm here on a Temporary Graduate (subclass 485) visa, which is government-speak for "we'll let you stay while you figure it out." I'm figuring it out. Slowly. Enthusiastically. With sprinkles.

This website isn't a highlight reel of a career I don't have yet — it's a celebration of the very real, very chaotic, very okay place I'm actually at. Ambitious, underemployed, over-degreed, and having a genuinely great time anyway.
